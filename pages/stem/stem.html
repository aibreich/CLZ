Intro to STEM

Science Projects

Want to build a rocket and launch its way up in the air? Want to program a robot to navigate the hospital? Do you want to build an epic Lego City or a catapult or make explosive Instant snow? Call down or visit the zone and ask about STEM Activities. We Can bring them up to you or you can do them in the Zone. Rocket launches subject to weather.

3D Printing

We Have 3D printers for patients to use! Subject to material constraints, find something cool on thingiverse.com or design your own model on Tinkercad.com and we can print it for you. To learn more, call us or ask during a visit!

Telepresence Robots

If you can't get out of your room, try driving one of our first-person robots. Once you connect, you can drive the robot anywhere around the Campus without even leaving your bed. You see that the robot sees and the screen on the robot shows your face so you can interact with people the people the robot comes across.
