Video Games
You can game at Riley with our Minecraft realm, game tournaments and console checkout.

Device Checkout

During your stay you can check out one of our gaming devices to play with in your room! Subject to availability, we have VR headsets, Xboxes, PlayStation, Nintendo Switches and DS's you can pick from!

Ask your Child Life Specialist or call us

Minecraft Realm

Join our minecraft realm and play with friends!

How to Connect
To play Minecraft, you need a device with Minecraft on it like a phone, tablet, Xbox, Nintendo switch, windows computer or PlayStation.

If you don't have a device setup to play, Contact the ChildLife Zone or you child life specialist, we might have a device you can check out!
Rules
1: The Riley Realm is Only for Patients and their Parents to play on.

2: Do not steal or break other people's things.

2: Don't be mean or bully other players.

You will be removed from the realm after discharge.
Call the Child Life Zone/Tech Studio if you need help!
